Powerlifting Program

Weeks 5-8

Workout A - Monday: Squats and Assistance Work

  • Squat: 4 sets of 3-4 reps @ 80-85% of your 1RM
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squat: 3 sets of 8-10 reps per leg
  • Plank: 3 sets of 45-75 seconds
  • Stretching and cool down

Workout B - Wednesday: Bench Press, Overhead Press, and Assistance Work

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 3-4 reps @ 80-85% of your 1RM
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps per arm
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Stretching and cool down

Workout C - Friday: Deadlifts and Assistance Work

  • Deadlift: 4 sets of 3-4 reps @ 80-85% of your 1RM
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps per side
  • Stretching and cool down

Weeks 9-12: Intermediate Phase

October 9 - November 5, 2023

Workout A - Monday: Lower Body Focus

  • Squat: 4 sets of 4 reps @ 80-85% of 1RM
  • Deadlift: 3 sets of 3 reps @ 75-80% of 1RM
  • Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squat: 3 sets of 8 reps per leg
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 6-8 reps

Workout B - Wednesday: Upper Body Focus

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 4 reps @ 80-85% of 1RM
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 6 reps
  • Barbell Row: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets of 10 reps

Workout C - Friday: Full Body & Accessory

  • Front Squat: 3 sets of 5 reps
  • Sumo Deadlift: 3 sets of 3 reps
  • Incline Bench Press: 3 sets of 6 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps
